Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

What's for Dinner? ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Photos: When you use the app to snap photos of your ingredients, these images are processed to identify ingredients and generate recipe suggestions. Photos are not stored on our servers after processing.

Saved Recipes: Recipes you choose to save are stored locally on your device.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience. This does not include personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To identify ingredients from your photos and suggest recipes
  • To save your favorite recipes for offline access
  • To improve the app and fix bugs

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services for image processing and recipe generation. These services process data according to their own privacy policies and do not retain your photos.

Data Storage

Your saved recipes and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not maintain user accounts or store personal data on external servers.

Your Choices

  • You can delete saved recipes at any time within the app
  • You can deny camera permissions, though this limits app functionality
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes through the app or our website.
